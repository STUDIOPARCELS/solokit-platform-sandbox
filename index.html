How It Works

Three steps from signup to someone holding your card

1

Build your solokit

Add your projects, resume, skills, and contact info. Your portfolio lives at yourname.solokit.app — mobile-first, fast, and agent-ready.

2

Order your solocards

Physical rounded-square cards with your photo on front and a QR code on back. NFC chip embedded. Thick stock, premium feel. Made to be kept.

3

Hand them out

Someone taps or scans your solocard — your full portfolio opens instantly. No app needed. Works on every device. Update your portfolio anytime; every card you've handed out stays current.
